    How To Play:
  • Place ships/traps onto your board by clicking from the left panel and hovering over one of your ocean tiles.
  • Hit finish and begin hovering/clicking on enemy board to guess.
  • Hit will display a broken piece. A miss will show a splash of water.
  • First to destroy all ships wins the game! Good Luck!
